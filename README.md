# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior using transactional retail data to identify purchasing patterns, customer segments, product performance, and revenue drivers. The analysis combines Python, MySQL, and Power BI to deliver actionable business insights and recommendations.

The project follows a complete analytics workflow, including data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, SQL-based business analysis, dashboard development, and reporting.

---

## Objective

The objective of this project is to:

* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Analyze revenue across customer segments
* Evaluate subscription performance
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Assess the impact of discounts on purchasing decisions
* Provide data-driven business recommendations

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains 3,900 customer purchase transactions with 18 attributes covering customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and transaction details.

### Key Attributes

**Customer Information**

* Age
* Gender
* Location
* Subscription Status

**Purchase Information**

* Item Purchased
* Category
* Purchase Amount
* Season
* Size
* Color

**Behavioral Information**

* Review Rating
* Previous Purchases
* Shipping Type
* Discount Applied

## Business Questions

The project addresses the following questions:

1. Which gender generates the highest revenue?
2. Which customers spend above average while using discounts?
3. Which products receive the highest ratings?
4. How does spending differ by shipping type?
5. Do subscribers spend more than non-subscribers?
6. Which products are most dependent on discounts?
7. Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?
8. What are the most purchased products within each category?
9. Are repeat buyers more likely to subscribe?
10. Which age groups contribute the highest revenue?

---

## Key Findings

* Young Adults generated the highest overall revenue.
* Clothing emerged as the strongest-performing product category.
* Express shipping customers spent slightly more on average than standard shipping customers.
* Loyal customers represented the largest customer segment.
* Several products demonstrated strong dependence on discounts.
* Subscriber spending behavior was comparable to non-subscriber spending.

## Recommendations

### Subscription Growth

Introduce additional subscriber benefits to increase enrollment and retention.

### Customer Loyalty

Develop targeted loyalty programs to encourage repeat purchases and long-term engagement.

### Discount Optimization

Reduce reliance on discounts for products with established demand.

### Product Promotion

Prioritize top-rated and high-performing products in marketing campaigns.

### Targeted Marketing

Focus acquisition and retention efforts on high-revenue customer groups.
